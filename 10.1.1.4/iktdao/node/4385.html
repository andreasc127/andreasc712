西元二Ｏ一Ｏ年　十一月二十七日　英格蘭特爾福特姜氏佛堂
歲次庚寅十月二十二日 率性進修班恭求仙佛慈悲指示訓

People of great persuasion are here together
Hold to Your mission and fulfil it forever
Illuminate others and make the world better
Lead a peaceful life and lay the disaster
Assimilate into the Tao field you enter
Nurse the hope of lighting up the sky brighter
Trust in Holy Mater and be a Tao transmitter
Have a broad mind and a great character
Remedy the injustice and spread the truth over
Open your heart and receive the heavenly order
Push ahead with holy work and be a sticker
You are ready on the boat to cross the river
